Seek Answer to Exam question

Use Case Title: Student seeks answer to an Exam question
Use Case ID: UC6
Primary Actor: Student
Goal in Context: A student requires an answer to an exam question. The student posts this exam question with a view to receiving suggested answers from the community of fellow students.
Scope: Educational – The overall exam question posting forum as seen by all of the Exam Overflow community.

Stakeholders and Interests:
  • Student: Student wants to receive an answer to an exam question.
Preconditions:
  • The students account has not been barred from the system.
Main Success Scenario:
    1. System: The system requests the module for the specific Exam Question.
    2. Student: The student enters the module for the specific Exam Question.
    3. System: The system requests the year of the Exam the Question is from.
    4. Student: The student enters the year of the year the Exam Question is from.
    5. System: The system requests the topic of the Exam Question.
    6. Student: The student enters the topic relevant to the Exam Question.
    7. System: The system requests the Exam Question text from the student
    8. Student: The student enters the Exam Question Text.
    9. Student: The student clicks the "Submit" button to create the exam question.
    10. System: The system saves the exam question entered.
    11. System: The system displays a confirm message to the user that the exam question has been successfully uploaded.

    Extensions:
    1a. Student submits an exam question that already exists on the system.
    2a. System recognises that the exam question submitted already exists.
    3a. System does not upload the students exam’ question. Rather the system
    directs the student to the duplicate exam question originally posted on the forum and the associated answers.

    Post conditions:
    • The exam question has been stored in the system.
    • The exam question cannot be asked again in the future.
    • Students can now provide answers for the Exam Question.

    Priority: Medium
    Releases: One
    Frequency of Use: Many times after completion of college exams
    Channel to Primary Actor: System
