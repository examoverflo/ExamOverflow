Register Student

Use Case Title: Register Student
Use Case ID: UC5

A student wishes to sign up to use ExamOverflow. The system allows new students to create accounts by supplying the system with their personal details. The new student must provide a T-Number, Password, Username and Email address. The student types their details into the system and click ‘Sign Up’.

Alternative Scenario:
Some of the details entered do not meet the requirements for successful sign up. The T-Number does not begin with ‘T’ or does not contain 9 characters. The password does not meet the criteria required. The email address provided is already in use on the system. The system displays an error message.
