Seek Answer to General question

Use Case Title: Student seeks answer to a General question
Use Case ID: UC6
Primary Actor: Student
Goal in Context: A student requires an answer to a General question. The student posts this general question with a view to receiving suggested answers from the community of fellow students.
Scope: Educational â€“ The overall general question posting forum as seen by all of the Exam Overflow community.

Stakeholders and Interests:
  • Student: Student wants to receive an answer to a general question.
Preconditions:
  • The students account has not been barred from the system.
Main Success Scenario:
    1. System: The system requests the topic for the General Question.
    2. Student: The student enters the topic relevant to the General Question.
    3. System: The system requests the General Question text from the student
    4. Student: The student enters the General Question Text.
    5. Student: The student clicks the "Submit" button to create the general question.
    6. System: The system saves the general question entered.
    7. System: The system displays a confirm message to the user that the general question has been successfully uploaded.

    Extensions:
    1a. Student submits a general question that already exists on the system.
    2a. System recognises that the general question submitted already exists.
    3a. System does not upload the students general question. Rather the system
    directs the student to the duplicate general question originally posted on the forum and the associated answers.

    Post conditions:
    • The general question has been stored in the system.
    • The general question cannot be asked again in the future.
    • Students can now provide answers for the general Question.

    Priority: Medium
    Releases: One
    Frequency of Use: Many times after completion of college exams
    Channel to Primary Actor: System
