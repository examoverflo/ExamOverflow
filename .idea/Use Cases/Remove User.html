Use Case Title: Deny Disruptive Student Access

Use Case ID: 4

Primary Actor: Administrator

Secondary Actor: Disruptive Student

Goal in Context: An administrator wishes to deny a disruptive student access to the system.

Stakeholders and Interests

  • Administrator: Wants to deny a disruptive student access to the system
  • Disruptive Student: Wants to receive a email detailing why they have been barred from using the system

Preconditions: Administrator has rights to deny a student access to the system.
Administrator and disruptive student are members of system.
Disruptive student must not already be denied access.

Minimal Guarantess:

Trigger: Administrator removes access to system from a disruptive student

Main Success Scenario

  1. Administrator: Administrator clicks button to deny a disruptive student access.
  2. System: The system sends a email to the disruptive student detailing why they have been removed.

Alternative Flow

Post Condition:
Disruptive student can no longer access the system.

Priority: Low-Medium

Releases: Several

Frequency of Use: Low, maybe once a month

Channels to Primary Actor: Application

Channels to Secondary Actor: Application, Email

Open Isuses:

How are emails being sent.

Why would a Student need to be removed.
