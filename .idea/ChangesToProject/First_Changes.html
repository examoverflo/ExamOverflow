1) The introduction of 2 enums (topic + module)

2) The separation of General Questions and Exam Questions


1) We decided that to further the progress of our questions we could introduce two enums, topic for a general question
and module for an exam question. By doing this it makes questions easier to find and to ensure the question asked is in
the correct section. As well as this we believe it will make the sorting of general questions easier and ensure similar
questions can be found easily. This brought us to the idea of splitting general questions and exam questions, we decided
to do this to save the user time and to allow students to easily locate answers to past exam papers. In the exam question
there will be a module enum that will order the questions easily.


2) From looking at the question class we decided that it was very broad and will provide problems for users to find specific
questions. As well as this we realised we could add an extra feature, as it is students using ExamOverflow we came to the plan
to introduce a specific exam question feature. This will make it easier for students to look through past exam questions and easily
locate the exam question from a specific year, by using the exam year and the newly introduced module.
